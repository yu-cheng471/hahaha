About Me

I'm 17 years old and currently studying at Sunflower Trilingual School. Growing up in a trilingual environment has given me a strong academic foundation and good learning habits that I truly value.

Since childhood, I've been fascinated by how technology works. I was always curious about how machines move, how systems are made, and how smart devices connect people to the world. My parents encouraged me to ask questions, explore, and learn from mistakes, which inspired me to pursue engineering.

In high school, I took a class on engineering and innovation where I learned programming, design thinking, and problem-solving. One of the most memorable projects was creating a website for a small company that helped them calculate employee salaries more efficiently. Seeing my work solve real problems made me realize that technology is about helping people in daily life.

I've also participated in school activities, including working as a trilingual host (Chinese, English, Thai) for our school's 20th-anniversary event, which improved my communication and leadership skills.

Outside the classroom, I enjoy badminton and cycling, which teach me perseverance and teamwork.

I'm a curious and open-minded learner excited to design smart systems that make life easier and better for people in the future.
